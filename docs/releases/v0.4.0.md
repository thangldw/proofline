# Proofline v0.4.0

`v0.4.0` adds reviewable temporal relationships between evidence-backed decisions.

## Added

- Migration 15 introduces typed decision relations: `supersedes`, `implements`, `contradicts`,
  `based_on`, and `considered`.
- Relation creation and timeline APIs retain validity periods and auditable before/after state.
- A superseding transition atomically marks the previous decision obsolete and closes its
  `valid_to`; historical evidence remains available until its source is explicitly deleted.
- The memory UI can open a decision timeline without hiding its exact source evidence.
- Read-only contradiction/staleness candidates flag review needs without changing accepted memory.

## Retrieval

Search demotes sources whose current decision is obsolete or has ended validity. Current and
neutral evidence otherwise keep their normal lexical or semantic relevance ordering.

## Boundaries

Portable schema v1 preserves the underlying memories, validity fields, audit events, and source
evidence but does not yet serialize relation rows. Portable relation transfer will ship only with
an explicit schema migration and backward-compatible verifier.
