One inspectable path

From approved source to reviewed decision memory.

The workflow is deterministic before any model is involved. Unknown, deleted, cross-workspace, or hash-mismatched evidence fails closed instead of becoming an uncited answer.

  1. 01 · INPUTApproved sourceMarkdown, notes, registered folders, or local Git.
  2. 02 · PRESERVEImmutable versionStable identity, content hash, offsets, and exact lines.
  3. 03 · RETRIEVEEvidence setLexical or optional hybrid retrieval with visible lineage.
  4. 04 · REVIEWHuman gateAccept, reject, revise, or inspect the supporting quote.
  5. 05 · REMEMBERGoverned outcomeAnswer, decision, study item, proposal, or Studio artifact.

Colors identify semantic roles and every state is also labelled; the flow never relies on color alone.
