One inspectable chain

From source to decision, without losing the proof.

Proofline preserves identity at every transformation. Unknown, deleted, cross-workspace, out-of-range, or hash-mismatched evidence is rejected instead of becoming an unsupported claim.

  1. 01 · INPUTApproved sourceMarkdown, notes, registered folders, or local Git.
  2. 02 · PRESERVEImmutable versionStable identity, content hash, offsets, and exact lines.
  3. 03 · DERIVETransformationVersioned operation with explicit parent nodes.
  4. 04 · REVIEWHuman decisionAccept, reject, or revise with the evidence visible.
  5. 05 · PACKAGEVerifiable memoryPortable DAG, root hash, and content-free verification.

Colors identify semantic roles and every state is also labelled; the flow never relies on color alone.

Decision Evidence Package

A portable receipt for the whole decision.

Each source version, chunk, citation, transformation, artifact, and review is a hashed node. Their parent-child relationships form a deterministic Merkle DAG with one root hash.

Package contents

Source hashesExact citation spans Transformation versionParent-child links Review outcomeRoot hash

The package proves internal integrity and lineage. It does not prove author identity or authenticity; signatures and trust management are a later milestone.

Verification workflow

Inspect, verify, and compare without opening the source database.

Evidence packages are deterministic JSON or ZIP files. Verification runs locally and reports content-free failure codes.
