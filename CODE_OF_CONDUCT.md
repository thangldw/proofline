# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and specific. Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct,
doxxing, and deliberate disruption are not accepted in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove harmful content and restrict participation when necessary. Report
conduct concerns privately to `thangldw@gmail.com`. Include only the context required to investigate
the incident and do not attach private Proofline data.

This policy applies to repository discussions, reviews, issues, releases, and project-related public
representation.
